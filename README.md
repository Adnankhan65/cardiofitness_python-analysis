# 🏃‍♂️ Cardio Fitness Customer Analysis (Treadmill Data)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes customer data from a fitness equipment company to understand **customer demographics, product preferences, and usage behavior** for treadmill purchases.

The analysis is performed using **Python (Pandas)** to answer key business questions and generate **actionable insights** for improving customer targeting and product strategy.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

The goal of this project is to:

* Identify **most popular treadmill models**
* Understand **customer profile (age, income, gender, education)**
* Analyze **usage behavior and fitness levels**
* Discover relationships between **customer attributes and product selection**
* Provide **data-driven recommendations** for business growth

---

## 📊 Dataset Summary

* Total Records: **180 customers**
* Products: **3 treadmill models (TM195, TM498, TM798)**
* Features:

  * Age, Gender, Education
  * Marital Status
  * Income
  * Usage (days/week)
  * Fitness Level
  * Miles (expected usage)

---

## 📈 Key Insights

### 🛍️ Product Insights

* **TM195 is the most sold model (80 customers)**
* Followed by TM498 and TM798

📌 Insight: Entry-level model (TM195) dominates → strong demand for affordable products

---

### 👥 Customer Demographics

#### Gender Distribution

* Male customers: **104**
* Female customers: **76**

📌 Insight: Males are slightly higher → opportunity to grow female segment

---

#### Age Analysis

* Average age: **~29 years**
* Majority customers fall between **24–33 years**

📌 Insight: Core target audience = **young working professionals**

---

#### Income Analysis

* Average income: **~53K**
* Range: **29K – 104K**

📌 Insight:

* Lower income → entry-level models
* Higher income → premium models

---

#### Education

* Average education: **~15 years**

📌 Insight: Customers are relatively educated → awareness-driven purchases

---

### 🏃 Customer Behavior

#### Usage

* Average usage: **3–4 days/week**

#### Miles

* Average miles: **~103 miles**

📌 Insight: Customers are **regular users**, not occasional buyers

---

#### Fitness Level

* Average fitness rating: **3.3 (moderate)**

📌 Insight: Target segment = **moderately fit individuals (mass market)**

---

### 💍 Marital Status

* Both Single and Partnered customers contribute significantly

📌 Insight: No strong dependency → wide customer base

---

## 🔗 Relationship Analysis

### 💰 Income vs Product

* High income → **TM798 (Premium)**
* Mid/low income → **TM195**

📌 Insight: Clear **price-based segmentation**

---

### 🎂 Age vs Product

* Younger customers → TM195
* Mid-age → TM498
* Older/high income → TM798

📌 Insight: Product preference changes with age

---

### 💪 Fitness vs Product

* Higher fitness → TM798
* Moderate fitness → TM195 / TM498

---

### 🎓 Education vs Product

* Higher education slightly correlates with premium models

---

### 👤 Gender vs Product

* No strong impact, but males slightly dominate across models

---

### 💍 Marital Status vs Product

* Minimal effect on product choice

---

### 👨‍👩‍👧 Age Group Segmentation

* 18–25 → Entry-level (TM195)
* 25–35 → Mid-range (TM498)
* 35+ → Premium (TM798)

---

### 🔄 Combined Effect (Age + Income + Education)

* Customers with:

  * Higher income
  * Higher education
  * Age > 30

➡️ Prefer **premium model (TM798)**

📌 Insight: Strong segmentation opportunity

---
